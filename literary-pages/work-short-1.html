Loving the Whole Heart

By, Gracie Chattin

Loving a broken heart 

Is loving a heart of sorrow: 

It's a life fallen apart 

With no hope for tomorrow. 

Loving a broken heart, 

Is following a wandering lonely soul 

Down a dark and narrow path 

With nowhere else to go. 

Take my hand; I'll lead you through the thickets and the thorns

That scratch and tear at the place in you most worn.

Loving a healing heart  

Feels like running a race you might lose, 

But you 've already passed the start- 

Now you just have to make it through. 

Loving a healing heart  

Is holding a hand tight, 

But giving them room 

To piece themselves together inside. 

Loving a heart no longer broken but still not fully whole, is loving 

A growing heart that's filled with hope, and stronger than it knows. 

Gracie Chattin
